Overcome life’s challenges, improve mental health and achieve personal goals.

At CMH Counselling and psychotherapy we provide individually tailored therapeutic treatment to help you thrive and reach your full potential. Our Therapists are trained to the highest standards, offering confidential support and guidance to help you along your journey. Your wellbeing is of upmost importance to us and we will go above and beyond to ensure your mental and emotional wellbeing is looked after in our care.

Concessions may be considered for those on a low income.
